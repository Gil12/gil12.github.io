Privacy Policy – Cook.it

Last Updated: April 13, 2025

Overview

Cook.it ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. This policy is compliant with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, including data collection transparency and user consent.

Data We Collect and Why

Data Type Purpose Shared With Third Parties?
Photos (Uploaded or Taken in App) To identify ingredients and generate recipes Yes – Sent to Google Generative AI (Gemini API) for processing
Device Identifiers (e.g. IDFA) To serve relevant ads and track app performance Yes – Shared with Google for ads and analytics
App Usage Data To improve app features and fix bugs Yes – Aggregated with analytics providers (Google)
Crash Reports & Diagnostics To maintain app stability and resolve issues Yes – Sent to analytics tools

We do not collect personal names, email addresses, contact info, health data, or precise location.

Photo Handling

  • Photos are sent temporarily to Google's Generative AI API for ingredient recognition.
  • Cook.it does not permanently store your photos.
  • Google may retain data in accordance with their privacy practices.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide AI-powered recipe generation
  • Understand user behavior to improve functionality
  • Serve personalized and relevant ads
  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Google Gemini API – for AI image analysis
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK – for personalized ads

Each third-party service may collect and process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

User Privacy Controls

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of personalized ads via device settings (Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising)
  • Withdraw photo permissions at any time through your device settings

We honor "Limit Ad Tracking" and App Tracking Transparency (ATT) requirements.

Data Security

We use secure technologies and encryption to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and you use the app at your own risk.

Children's Privacy

Cook.it is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. We'll notify users by updating this page and revising the "Last Updated" date.
